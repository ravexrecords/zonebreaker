About the Mod

The Ultimate Post-Apocalyptic Experience

ZONEBREAKER is a comprehensive modpack that transforms your game into a gritty, unforgiving post-apocalyptic wasteland. With over 300 weapons, hardcore survival mechanics, and immersive environmental storytelling, every moment is a fight for survival.

What Makes ZONEBREAKER Special

• 300+ Weapons: From makeshift pipe rifles to advanced energy weapons
• Hardcore Survival: Radiation, hunger, thirst, and disease mechanics
• Dynamic Weather: Toxic storms and radioactive rain
• Faction Warfare: Choose your allies wisely in the wasteland
• Base Building: Fortify your position against the hostile world
